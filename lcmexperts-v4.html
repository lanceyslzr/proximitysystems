The Authority

A Nurse Who Built
the Field's Playbook.

Jennifer spent 15 years as an ICU charge nurse at Logan Health, served as a U.S. Peace Corps volunteer in Papua New Guinea, and trained in critical care surgical nursing at Detroit Medical Center before founding The Life Care Experts in 2006.

"I built frameworks that I've been using for 20 years — and made them available for anyone."

She co-developed Montana's State Plan for Alzheimer's and Related Dementias, serves on the BOLD Steering Committee, has provided expert witness testimony in deposition and trial settings, and has trained hundreds of care professionals across Montana since 2019.
