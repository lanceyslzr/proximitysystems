Our Services

Care that fits
your loved one's
daily life.

Every care plan is tailored to the individual — building on their strengths, honoring their routines, and supporting their independence at home.

01

Personal Care

Assistance with bathing, grooming, dressing, and personal hygiene — delivered with dignity, patience, and genuine care.

02

Companionship

Meaningful connection and social engagement to combat loneliness. A cheerful presence that brightens every visit.

03

Meal Preparation

Nutritious, home-cooked meals prepared to your loved one's preferences and dietary needs — with care and a personal touch.

04

Light Housekeeping

A clean, safe, and comfortable home environment — laundry, dishes, tidying, and more so your loved one can focus on living.

05

Transportation and Errands

Safe transportation to appointments, social activities, and errands — keeping your loved one connected to their community.

06

Medication Reminders

Timely reminders and support to ensure medications are taken on schedule — giving families peace of mind every day.

About Abdullah

Built by someone
who has been inside
the care world.

Abdullah Mahmud is the Founder and CEO of Cheerful Moments Home Care — a non-medical home care agency serving families across the Bay Area in California.

Before founding Cheerful Moments, Abdullah served as Memory Care Director and Care Director at Aegis Living, and as a Client Care Coordinator at Comfort Keepers. He brings that frontline experience to every client relationship.

His mission is simple — to provide compassionate, high-quality care to those in need, while building an agency that families trust and caregivers are proud to work for.
