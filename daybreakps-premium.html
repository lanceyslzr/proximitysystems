About Pamela

Not a Franchise.
A Family.

In 2003, Pamela Dagenhart founded DayBreak Personal Services in Oak Ridge, Tennessee with a simple belief — that elderly and disabled adults deserve to stay in their own homes, on their own terms, with people who genuinely care.

"Now a way to reclaim your time — time to spend with your family, friends, or yourself."

Twenty-two years later, DayBreak is still locally owned, still run by Pamela, and still built on that same foundation. No corporate turnover. No franchise playbook. Just a dedicated team with over 100 years of combined caregiving experience serving Anderson, Roane, and Knox Counties with a 1-to-1 worker to client ratio that ensures every client gets the personal attention they deserve.
