Our Story

A Nurse Who Saw
What Was Missing.

For years, Shawndra Quinn RN worked inside nursing homes and home health agencies across Indiana. She watched families stretched thin, seniors longing for the comfort of home, and systems that prioritized volume over dignity.

"Home isn't just a place — it's where we feel safest, happiest, and most like ourselves."

That realization became Enchanted Hearts. A nurse-owned agency built not on policy, but on personal experience — caring for her own loved ones and understanding what families truly need. With over two decades of medical expertise, Shawndra built the agency she wished had existed all along.
