My Story

From corporate
America to full-time
caregiver.

Imagine going from a corporate career to becoming a full-time caregiver for both parents struggling with different forms of dementia — plus a mother-in-law with Alzheimer's. That happened to me.

It completely rocked my world. And when it happened, I needed ideas, solutions, strategies, and tools. I couldn't find the useful information I needed — and I don't want that to happen to you.

My experience has turned into my passion. I now help others who find themselves in the same position — dealing daily with the challenges that caregiving brings.

Can you relate to any of these?

You have aging parents who need help but you don't know where to begin.

Your assisted living facility is constantly dealing with turnover of your staff.

You are a caregiver completely burned out or stressed on a daily basis with no support.

You struggle with the differences between Alzheimer's, other dementia types, and normal aging.

You need education sessions and resources for your organization or group.

About Debbie

Turning personal
pain into purpose
for others.

Debbie Compton is a Certified Caregiving Coach, bestselling author, speaker, and Alzheimer's educator based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.

Her journey began when she left corporate America to care for both parents with dementia and a mother-in-law with Alzheimer's. That experience became her mission — to make sure no caregiver has to face it alone.

Today she helps caregivers, healthcare organizations, corporate teams, and assisted living facilities build the tools, knowledge, and resilience they need to keep going.
