07 — Security & Compliance
Your clients' data is
treated like it belongs to them.
You are dealing with some of the most sensitive information that exists — elderly clients, health conditions, home addresses, family contacts. Proximity OS was built with that responsibility in mind from day one.
PHIPA-Conscious Design
All AI processing anonymizes client data before it leaves your system. Names, addresses, and health details are stripped from AI calls. Raw PHI never touches third-party models.
Session-Based Authentication
Every portal requires secure authentication. HQ uses server-side session tokens. Family and PSW portals use PIN-based access. No shared passwords.
Canadian Data Hosting
Data stored in Supabase with Canadian data residency options. Each operator's data is fully isolated — your clients are never visible to other agencies on the platform.
CASL-Compliant Email
Every email includes the required CASL footer, unsubscribe link, and your physical business address. Automated follow-up stays on the right side of Canadian anti-spam law.
Rate Limiting & Bot Protection
All public endpoints are rate-limited and honeypot-protected. Brute force attempts are blocked automatically. Security headers are A-rated on independent testing.
127-Point Live Health Console
The system monitors itself continuously — 127 active health checks running at all times. If anything fails, it surfaces immediately. You are never the last to know.
