The Challenge

Running Two Operations Manually Is the Problem

A retirement residence and a home care operation running simultaneously means double the admin, double the leads to chase, and double the billing to manage. Without automation, something always falls through.

⏱️
Leads Falling Through
Families inquiring about residency or home care call multiple operators at once. The first to respond wins. Manual follow-up means missed revenue across both verticals.
Lost Revenue
📋
Admin Across Two Operations
Timesheets, invoices, resident intake forms, staff scheduling — all done manually across two separate businesses. That is 20+ hours every month that should run itself.
Time Drain

After-Hours Inquiries
