The Approach

Care that feels like
family.

Seniors Helping Seniors is built on a simple idea — seniors helping seniors. Lara's team brings warmth, lived experience, and genuine companionship to every home visit.

01

Non-medical, professional care

Comprehensive in-home support that goes beyond tasks — building real relationships with clients and families based on trust and consistency.

02

Healthcare-informed leadership

With 23 years as a PharmD, Lara brings clinical understanding and regulatory expertise to every aspect of how care is delivered and documented.

03

Focused on independence

The goal is always to help seniors stay in their own homes, on their own terms — empowered, safe, and supported by people who genuinely care.

04

Family kept in the loop

Families stay informed and at ease through regular updates and open communication — so everyone feels connected, wherever they are.

Services

Everything seniors
need at home.

From daily companionship to practical household help — Seniors Helping Seniors Buckhead covers every non-medical need so your loved one can stay comfortably at home.

01

Companionship

Genuine connection and conversation — visits that brighten the day, reduce isolation, and give families peace of mind knowing someone they trust is there.

02

Personal Care Assistance

Dignified help with daily living activities — grooming, dressing, bathing, and mobility support delivered with patience, respect, and warmth.

03

Transportation

Safe, reliable rides to medical appointments, errands, social activities, and family visits — keeping seniors connected to the life they love.

04

Errands and Grocery

Shopping, pharmacy pickups, and everyday errands handled with care — so seniors have what they need without the stress or physical strain of doing it alone.

05

Hospital Discharge Support

Smooth, supported transitions from hospital to home — ensuring seniors arrive safely, are settled comfortably, and have everything they need to recover well.

06

Medication Reminders

Gentle, consistent reminders to take medications as prescribed — backed by Lara's PharmD expertise and deep commitment to medication safety for seniors.

About Lara

A pharmacist who
chose to care
differently.

Lara Smith PharmD spent 23 years as a pharmacist at Publix Pharmacy — building deep expertise in healthcare, medication management, regulatory compliance, and working directly with senior patients every day.

In 2025, she channeled that experience into Seniors Helping Seniors Buckhead — bringing clinical knowledge and genuine passion for senior wellbeing to an in-home care business that is truly different from the rest.

Featured by the Brookhaven Chamber of Commerce as part of their Women-Owned Business Spotlight, Lara is building something that matters — and families across Buckhead, Brookhaven, and Chamblee are already feeling it.
