The Approach

Transitions handled
with care and expertise.

Whether a senior is staying home or moving to a supportive environment, Deb ensures the process is smooth, stress-free, and deeply respectful of the family's needs at every step.

01

Comprehensive family support

Deb works with the whole family — not just the senior — to ensure everyone is informed, comfortable, and confident throughout the transition.

02

Tailored to individual needs

No two transitions are the same. Every plan is personalised — whether the goal is aging in place with support or moving to a community that truly fits.

03

Real estate expertise included

Deb's background in Quad Cities real estate means she understands the home selling side of the transition as deeply as the care side — a rare combination.

04

Dementia-informed care

As a Certified Dementia Friend and caregiver to her own mother, Deb brings lived experience and deep empathy to every family navigating cognitive decline.

Services

Every detail.
Taken care of.

From the first conversation to the final step of a transition — Deb manages every detail so families can focus on what matters most: each other.

01

Decluttering and Downsizing

Compassionate support sorting through a lifetime of belongings — what to keep, donate, sell, or let go — at a pace that feels manageable and respectful.

02

Placement Assistance

Finding the right supportive community or care environment that truly fits the senior's needs, preferences, and personality — not just what's available.

03

Age in Place Assessments

A thorough assessment of what's needed to help a senior stay safely and comfortably in their own home — with a personalised care needs plan to match.

04

Home Selling Expertise

Full real estate support for seniors and families selling the family home — with deep Quad Cities market knowledge and sensitivity to the emotional weight of the process.

05

Dementia Support

Certified Dementia Friend with personal caregiver experience — bringing genuine understanding and empathy to families navigating the challenges of cognitive decline.

06

Senior Home Coaching

One-on-one guidance for seniors and their families — helping navigate options, make confident decisions, and feel supported through every stage of the journey.

About Deb

Helping families
through life's biggest
transitions.

Deb Haussmann is a Senior Home Coach and Certified Dementia Friend based in Bettendorf, Iowa — providing comprehensive, compassionate support for seniors and their families through major life transitions across the Quad Cities.

Her approach covers every dimension of a senior transition — from decluttering and placement to age-in-place care assessments and home selling. Her background in Quad Cities real estate at Keller Williams means she understands both the care side and the practical side of what families face.

Having been the caregiver for her own mother through dementia, Deb brings lived experience and genuine empathy to every family she works with. She knows this journey firsthand — and she walks it with you.
