What We Offer

Everything your
loved ones need.

Our Myfolksers are vetted, compassionate individuals who can help with a wide range of practical and emotional needs — booked easily through our app or by phone.

01

Companionship

A friendly face, a good chat, a cuppa and connection. Combating the loneliness that is proven to be as harmful as smoking 15 cigarettes a day.

02

Hospital Discharge

Support when your loved one comes home from hospital — settling in, reassurance, and ensuring the transition feels safe and comfortable.

03

Household Support

Shopping, errands, helping with the TV, tidying up — the small everyday things that make an enormous difference to how someone feels at home.

04

Technology Support

Help with phones, tablets, the TV remote, and navigating technology — keeping your loved one connected to family, services, and the world.

05

Sitting Service

A Myfolkser stays with your loved one so you can take a break, attend an appointment, or simply rest — knowing they are in safe, caring hands.

06

External Visits

Accompanying your loved one to appointments, activities, and outings — maintaining their connection to the community and the world outside.

About Louise

Built from
lived experience,
paid forward.

Louise Thompson created Myfolks from the most personal of places. Two elderly parents in Newcastle. Louise in Sussex. 325 miles apart. The help they needed was simple — someone to fix the TV, answer a confusing letter, take them to an appointment — but nothing like this existed.

So she built it. A 25-year career in legal and commercial leadership at blue-chip tech companies (EDS, BT, SAP) gave her the tools. Her parents gave her the reason.

Today, Myfolks is funded by Capita, backed by Innovate UK, and expanding rapidly across the UK — helping families like Louise's give their loved ones the support they deserve.
