The Founder's Story

She Left Corporate America.
Love Called Her Home.

Rosalind C. Bland spent over 15 years in corporate America — a BS in Accounting, a Masters in Psychology with a focus in Crisis Counseling, a career built on discipline and excellence. Then her mother passed away, leaving behind her father in the advanced stages of Alzheimer's.

"Ask God to show you what you are called to do." — Rosalind's late mother

Rosalind brought her father home. She hired caregivers. The experience left a lot to be desired. On October 31, 2013, her father passed. And out of that grief, she found her calling — to build the kind of care she wished her father had received.

Abundant Love Home Care Services was born that year. Today it serves nine counties across North Carolina, operates out of two locations in Charlotte and Hickory, and has touched the lives of clients from four continents. This is not just a business. It is a labor of love.
