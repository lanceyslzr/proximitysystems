How It Works

Three steps to transform care.

Echobox is simple by design. It takes minutes to set up and immediately changes how caregivers connect with those in their care.

01
👤
Administrator Creates an Account
Click 'Join for FREE' at echobox.ca to become the Administrator. Add and invite Caregivers, list Storytellers — those in care. Free accounts include one Caregiver, one Storyteller, and one Story.
02
📱
Caregivers Download the App
Invited Caregivers receive a link by email and download the Echobox Memory Vault App on their smartphone or tablet. They sign in, add Storytellers to their dashboard, and begin browsing Topics to capture Stories.
03
❤️
Capture Stories That Matter
Choose a Topic your Storyteller loves and capture a Story. Each Story holds 1,000 words, 3 photos, a video, and an audio recording. Upgrade to unlock unlimited Caregivers and Stories at just $30/month per Storyteller.
The Story Behind Echobox

Built by Tim & Tannis.

Tim and Tannis Roberts founded Echobox Memory Vault after witnessing something profound — a senior whose entire personality, history, and preferences were unknown to the caregivers serving them daily. The disconnect was heartbreaking. It didn't have to be that way.

"The better we know someone, the better care we can provide."

From Calgary, Alberta, Tim and Tannis have grown Echobox into a globally recognized platform — featured on BBC News, CTV News, showcased by dementia care expert Teepa Snow, and awarded $100K through Alberta Innovates. It launched globally in 2024 and continues to transform how care teams connect with the people they serve.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Does Echobox work offline?▾
Yes. The Echobox app works offline so caregivers can access stored stories and preferences even without an internet connection. Everything syncs when they reconnect.
Can family members add stories too?▾
Absolutely. Families can create their own private account and contribute memories, photos, and personal details from anywhere in the world — so loved ones are always represented, even when family can't be there in person.
What devices does it work on?▾
Echobox is available on iOS and Android smartphones and tablets. Administrators manage accounts at echobox.ca on any browser. No special equipment needed.
Is it private and secure?▾
Completely. Echobox is ad-free and built with the dignity of seniors in mind. Stories are private, never shared, and only accessible to the people you invite. Your loved one's memories stay where they belong.
What happens after the 90-day trial?▾
If you choose to continue, it's $30/month per Storyteller with unlimited caregivers. If you decide it's not right for you, there's no commitment and no charge. Tim and Tannis stand behind the product completely.
How long does it take to set up?▾
Under 5 minutes. Create an account, add a Storyteller, invite a Caregiver. The first story can be captured the same day. No training required — if you can use a smartphone, you can use Echobox.
