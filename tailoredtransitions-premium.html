Our Services

Every detail.
Taken care of.

From the first consultation to the final tote unpacked — that's right, we said tote — Tailored Transitions manages every part of your move so you don't have to.

01

Full Move Management

Complete coordination of your senior's move — planning, scheduling, vendor management, packing, moving, and unpacking. We handle everything from start to settled.

02

Downsizing Support

Compassionate help sorting through a lifetime of belongings — deciding what to keep, donate, sell, or let go of — at a pace that feels manageable and respectful.

03

Packing and Unpacking

Professional, careful packing of all belongings and complete unpacking and setup at the new home — so your loved one arrives to a space that already feels like home.

04

Space Planning and Setup

Thoughtful layout planning for the new space so furniture and belongings are placed with purpose — functional, safe, and familiar from day one.

About Amy

Helping families
through one of
life's biggest moves.

Amy Denstedt is the founder of Tailored Transitions — a senior move management company serving families across Waterloo Region with compassion, expertise, and genuine care.

With over 20 years of experience in customer success, senior engagement, and move management, Amy brings deep understanding to every family she works with. She knows that moving a parent isn't just logistics — it's one of the most emotionally significant transitions a family will navigate.

Amy is a member of the National Association of Senior and Specialty Move Managers (NASMM) and an active member of the Greater KW Chamber of Commerce — bringing trusted connections and community roots to every engagement.
