What We Do

The right person.
Right now.

From a single overnight shift to long-term cover arrangements — Care Response 24/7 places experienced, vetted care workers across Scotland at any hour.

01

Home Care Staffing

Experienced carers placed in clients' homes at short notice — personal care, daily support, and companionship whenever your team needs backup.

02

Care Home Cover

Qualified, vetted care home staff available on-call — filling shifts at any hour to ensure your residents receive consistent, high-quality care every day.

03

Support Work

Skilled support workers for people with learning disabilities, mental health needs, and complex care requirements — placed with urgency and care.

04

Temp to Permanent

Short-term placements that can convert to permanent contracts after 12 weeks or 420 hours — a proper fit before committing long-term.

05

In-House Academy

Ongoing training above basic requirements — developing skills and increasing opportunities for every worker on our books across Scotland.

06

24/7 On-Call Support

A real person at the end of the phone every hour of every day — not a system, not a voicemail. Always ready to respond to urgent staffing needs.

About Jayne

Leading with
care at the centre.

Jayne Anderson is the Area Director at Care Response 247 LTD — bringing extensive experience in healthcare staffing, employment pathways, and community services to everything the organisation does across Scotland.

Her background spans The Wise Group, ENABLE Scotland, Remploy, and Brightwork — giving her deep understanding of both the workforce and the communities that depend on quality care being available when it's needed most.

Jayne leads with the same person-first philosophy that defines Care Response 24/7 — ensuring both workers and clients receive the attention, support, and respect they deserve at every step.
