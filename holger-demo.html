What We Provide

Every level of care. In your home.

From a few hours of companionship to 24-hour care, we match your loved one with the right caregiver for their needs and personality.

Personal Care

Bathing, grooming, dressing, and daily hygiene provided with dignity and respect. We only hire caregivers we would trust with our own families.

Dementia Care

Specialized support for Alzheimer's and all stages of dementia using the Meaningful Day program. We support the whole family, not just the client.

Companionship

Meaningful social interaction, conversation, activities, and outings. Reduces isolation and supports mental health. More than care — genuine human connection.

Post-Hospital Recovery

Transitional care after surgery or hospital discharge. We coordinate with your medical team to reduce re-admission risk and support a safe recovery at home.

Overnight and 24-Hour Care

Round-the-clock support for seniors who need continuous care. From overnight stays to full-time live-in arrangements with consistent, reliable coverage every shift.

Respite Care

Relief for family caregivers who need to rest or attend to other obligations. We step in so you can recharge, knowing your loved one is safe and cared for.

Meal Preparation

Nutritious, balanced meal planning and preparation tailored to dietary needs and preferences. Supports physical health and establishes comforting daily routines.

Medication Reminders

Non-medical medication reminders to ensure schedules are followed. Caregivers monitor regularity without administering medications, keeping loved ones on track safely.

Light Housekeeping

Laundry, cleaning, errands, and grocery shopping to maintain a safe, comfortable home environment. Supports independence while reducing daily stress on seniors and families.

Your Local Owner

Built for this community.

Holger Fischer is a Kitchener-Waterloo resident and the owner of Always Best Care of Kitchener-Waterloo. His goal is simple — to join your family's journey and provide the best possible care plan for your loved one.

Holger has spent his life giving back to the Waterloo Region community. From Scouts Canada since his youth, to working as a lifeguard and Water Safety Instructor at university, to coaching and refereeing wrestling, his commitment to others has been lifelong. That same commitment is what drives everything at Always Best Care of Kitchener-Waterloo.

Every caregiver on Holger's team is carefully screened, thoroughly trained, and insured before their first shift. His team has built strong relationships with area hospitals, social workers, and senior communities across the region. When you call, you reach real people who know your community.
