Our Mission

Accessible care
for those who
need it most.

The Ann Silverman Community Health Clinic exists to bridge the gap — providing accessible medical, dental, and behavioral services to community members who would otherwise go without.

"Not every moment of care needs more activity. Sometimes, it needs more understanding."

Meri L. Gaumond · Executive Director

Medical Services

Comprehensive primary care services for qualified community members — delivered with dignity, compassion, and clinical excellence.

Dental Care

Accessible dental services for those who could not otherwise afford care — because oral health is foundational to overall wellbeing.

Behavioral Health

Mental health and behavioral services that meet people where they are — with empathy, evidence-based care, and deep respect for the whole person.

Community Outreach

Education, fundraising, and stakeholder engagement to advance the clinic's mission and deepen its impact across Doylestown and Bucks County.

Services

Care without
barriers.

The clinic's services are designed to remove the barriers that keep people from getting care — financial, geographic, and systemic. Every person who walks through the door deserves to be seen.

01

Primary Medical Care

Accessible primary care for qualified individuals and families — preventive, diagnostic, and treatment services delivered with clinical skill and human compassion.

02

Dental Services

Oral health care for community members who cannot afford private dental services — because a healthy mouth is fundamental to a healthy life.

03

Behavioral Health

Mental and behavioral health support for individuals and families navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, and life transitions — with dignity at the center of every session.

04

Dementia Education

Community education on Alzheimer's disease, dementia care, and caregiver support — helping families understand what their loved ones are experiencing and how to respond.

05

Caregiver Support

Resources, training, and guidance for family caregivers — because the people caring for others need care too, especially when memory and behavior change daily.

06

Volunteer Programs

Opportunities for community members to serve alongside the clinic team — contributing skills, time, and heart to a mission that makes a real difference in people's lives.

About Meri

Led by love.
Guided by faith.

Meri L. Gaumond is the Executive Director of the Ann Silverman Community Health Clinic in Doylestown, Pennsylvania — leading a team dedicated to providing accessible medical, dental, and behavioral health services to those in need.

With over 13 years at Pickering Manor as Administrator of Memory Care and Dementia Trainer, Meri brings deep expertise in dementia program development, policy, and care to everything she leads. She is the co-creator of DementiaBuddy and Founder of Dementia Care at Home and the International Dementia Care and Cure Nonprofit.

Her forthcoming book Led by Love, Guided by Faith (July 2026) reflects the spiritual core of her mission — that every act of care is an act of love.
