About the Clinic

Built for the Community.
Led by an Expert.

The Ann Silverman Community Health Clinic exists because access to quality healthcare should never depend on your zip code, income, or insurance status. Led by Meri L. Gaumond CDP, CADDCT, CDCM, PAC, the clinic bridges the gap between need and care.

"Healthcare that reaches those who need it most — that is not a service, it is a responsibility."

With over 21 years of experience and four specialized certifications including Certified Dementia Practitioner and Certified Alzheimer's Disease and Dementia Care Trainer, Meri brings clinical excellence and deep compassion to every patient interaction and community program she leads.

Comprehensive Care
Under One Roof

Dementia & Memory Care
